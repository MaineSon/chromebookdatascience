Chromebook Data Science is a free online educational program to help anyone who can read, write, and use a computer to move into data science, the number one rated job.

Why data science?

Data science is the number one rated job by Glassdoor and there are more than 350,000 new data science expected by 2020. .

Challenges

But people who are currently unemployed, or employed in retail or other low tech jobs face hurdles to getting data science jobs. The high cost of computers, education, and the fact that most jobs are in high-cost metro areas prevent people from getting into data science jobs.

Our approach


We minimize computer costs

We have developed an online program that lets students do data science using only a web browser and cheap computers like Chromebooks.

Learn more about Google Chromebook

Free courses

Our Chromebook Data Science program is entirely online and you can take it for free. You can take the classes any time and at your own pace. You get a certificate for each Johns Hopkins online class from Leanpub.

See our curriculum

Learn anywhere

Data science work can be done from anywhere there is an Internet connection. Chromebook Data Science can be completed from anywhere.

Finding jobs

Our program covers soft skills you need to get data science jobs, including how to network and get your name out there. We are also working with partner companies who need to fill data science roles to place our graduates in remote and local data science jobs. (If you are looking for data scientists consider becoming a Chromebook Data Science hiring partner).
